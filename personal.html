I was born in Mexico City and lived there until I was 10. My family and I then moved to the San Francisco Bay Area. Despite my love for the bay, I went to college in the Southern California city of Claremont at Pomona College and majored in Cognitive Science. For better or for worse (for better it seems), I went straight to grad school in Boulder and have been here ever since.
I love to explore urban environments, tinker with and ride bikes, eat spicy food, snowboard, rock climb, and stay up way too late doing inane things on the interwebs!
